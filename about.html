About Ek Yana

Ek Yana is a unique app designed to guide you on the path of Dharma, blending ancient wisdom with modern technology. Explore spiritual features, connect with tradition, and experience rituals in new ways.

Core Features

1. Virtual Pandit Ji (Live & AI-based)
Consult a real pandit via video call or interact with an AI Pandit Ji trained on authentic scriptures for instant mantras, rituals, and answers to dharma-related queries.
UI: Home screen card “Ask Pandit Ji” with tilak symbol. Choose Live Pandit (video call) or AI Assistant (chat). Animated idol/lamp background during interaction.
2. Pilgrimage Planner (Offline + AR Assistance)
Plan visits to major temples with maps, best times, local rituals, and offline navigation.
UI: Pilgrimage calendar with auspicious days in saffron. Tap temple → 3D AR model + offline map. “My Pilgrimage Bag” checklist for ritual items.
3. VR Temple Tours
Enjoy 360° immersive VR experiences of famous temples and sacred rivers.
UI: Mandala-shaped VR button. Swipe to switch temples; background chants play. “Aarti Mode” lets you virtually offer a diya.
4. Life-cycle Rituals Hub
Complete guidance for birth rites, thread ceremony, marriage, cremation, and shraddh rituals.
UI: Scrollable sacred tree timeline. Tap age/occasion → Ritual steps with images & audio. Option to “Book a Pandit”.
5. Daily Puja & Mantra Alarm
Alarm plays morning mantras, evening aarti, or custom chants.
UI: Circular alarm clock with diya flame animation. Tap flame to snooze, swipe up to offer flowers. Custom mantra library.
6. Digital Prasad & Donation Service
Book prasad delivery from temples or donate to dharmic causes.
UI: “Offer Now” button with animated flower falling into plate. Payment screen with temple images.

Exclusive & Confidential Concepts

A. Dharma Karma Ledger 🕉️ (Gamified Good Deeds Tracker)
Log daily good deeds (feeding animals, helping others, reciting mantras). Earn “Punya Points” based on Vedic principles. AI Pandit Ji gives a yearly spiritual report card with blessings & personalized dharmic growth advice.
Why it’s special: Tradition + gamification; authentic scripture-based karma tracking.
B. Ancestor VR Darshan & Digital Shraddh
Upload ancestor photos → AI restores them into peaceful images placed in a sacred VR mandapam with virtual rituals. Invite family worldwide to join VR shraddh together.
Why it’s special: Blends ancestral reverence with VR, enabling global participation in rites.
